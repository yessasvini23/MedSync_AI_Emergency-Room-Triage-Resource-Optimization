MedSync AI provides **AI-assisted decision support** by:

- Assigning emergency **triage levels**
- Estimating **risk of deterioration**
- Recommending the **best hospital** based on capacity and specialists
- Triggering **time-critical alerts** for cardiac, stroke, and trauma cases

## ğŸ§  How It Works

MedSync AI is powered by a **Golden Prompt** built in **Google AI Studio**.

Gemini acts as a **virtual emergency coordinator**, reasoning across:
- Patient vitals and symptoms

- Safety and escalation rules
